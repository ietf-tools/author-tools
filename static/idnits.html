I-D Nits (idnits)

This service inspects Internet-Draft (I-D) documents for conditions that should be adjusted to bring the document into line with policies from the IETF, the IETF Trust, and the RFC Editor. Issues are reported based on:

  • I-D Content Guidelines
  • Additional requirements captured from Area Directors (ADs) and authors over time.
  • Requirements in the original description of I-D Nits
Try idnits v3 with XML.
Provide a valid URL. See the About page for a list of allowed domains.
